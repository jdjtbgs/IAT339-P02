Branding Identity - Succulent

Style Guide

Succulent is a company that sells edible, natural cosmetics and beauty goods. The product is luxurious, sensual, indulgent, and healthy. Specifically, there is one line of lipstick, body butter, and perfume.

The company is down to earth, friendly, and a little sexually cheeky but classy.

Our product is health and environment conscious while also allowing consumers to indulge in luxurious beauty products that make them feel beautiful, sensual, and close to nature.
